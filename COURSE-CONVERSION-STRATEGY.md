# Course Conversion Strategy

## Overview

This course is designed to convert visitors into [Syllaby.io](https://syllaby.io/?via=chris56) users through value-first education and proven results.

---

## Conversion Funnel

### Stage 1: Awareness (Main README)
- **Hook**: "Complete Guide to Automating Faceless Video Content"
- **Value**: Free comprehensive guide + course
- **CTA**: "Start Course" or "Get Free Trial"
- **Affiliate Link**: Prominently placed

### Stage 2: Interest (Course Landing Page)
- **Hook**: "Transform from Beginner to Profitable in 4-6 Weeks"
- **Value**: 10 modules, bonuses, proven results
- **Social Proof**: Success stories, 150,000+ users
- **CTA**: "Get Syllaby.io Free Trial"
- **Affiliate Link**: Multiple placements

### Stage 3: Consideration (Module 0)
- **Hook**: "Welcome to Your Transformation"
- **Value**: Real success stories, clear outcomes
- **Social Proof**: Specific testimonials with results
- **CTA**: "Start Free Trial" (multiple times)
- **Affiliate Link**: In every lesson

### Stage 4: Action (Throughout Course)
- **Hook**: "Get Results in 2-3 Weeks"
- **Value**: Step-by-step actionable content
- **Social Proof**: Real metrics and timelines
- **CTA**: "Get Started" at end of each module
- **Affiliate Link**: Strategic placements

## Affiliate Link Placement Strategy

### High-Value Locations

1. **Main README** (Top of page)
   - "Get Syllaby.io Free Trial →"
   - Multiple CTAs throughout

2. **Course Landing Page**
   - Hero CTA
   - Prerequisites section
   - Quick Start section
   - Multiple strategic placements

3. **Module 0 (Welcome)**
   - Lesson 4: "Getting Started with Syllaby.io"
   - Lesson 5: "Setting Up Your Account"
   - Action items
   - End of module

4. **Every Module**
   - Resources section
   - Next steps
   - Action items
   - Natural context

5. **Guides**
   - Getting started guides
   - Feature guides
   - Monetization guides

### Link Context

**Best Practices**:
- ✅ Natural integration (not forced)
- ✅ Value-first (link after providing value)
- ✅ Clear benefit ("Free Trial", "Start Creating")
- ✅ Multiple placements (but not overwhelming)
- ✅ Contextual (relevant to content)

**Examples**:
- "Get your [Syllaby.io account](https://syllaby.io/?via=chris56) to start creating"
- "[Start your free trial →](https://syllaby.io/?via=chris56)"
- "Ready to get started? [Get Syllaby.io →](https://syllaby.io/?via=chris56)"

## Content Strategy

### Value-First Approach

**Every piece of content provides value BEFORE asking for action**:

1. **Education**: Teach something valuable
2. **Examples**: Show real results
3. **Action Items**: Give clear next steps
4. **CTA**: Natural call to action with link

### Content Flow

**Module Structure**:
1. Overview (what you'll learn)
2. Lessons (valuable content)
3. Action Items (engagement)
4. Resources (tools needed)
5. Next Steps (CTA with link)

**Guide Structure**:
1. Introduction (value)
2. How-to content (education)
3. Examples (proof)
4. Next Steps (CTA)
