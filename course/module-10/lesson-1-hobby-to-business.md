# Lesson 1: From Hobby to Business

**Duration**: 30 minutes  
**Goal**: Transform content creation into a real business

---

## Making the Transition

### Mindset Shift

**From Hobby**:
- Casual approach
- Inconsistent
- No systems
- No goals

**To Business**:
- Professional approach
- Consistent systems
- Clear goals
- Track metrics

---

## Business Setup

### 1. Set Up Business Structure

**Options**:
- Sole proprietorship (simple)
- LLC (protection)
- Corporation (advanced)

**Recommendation**: Start with sole proprietorship, upgrade to LLC as you scale

---

### 2. Separate Business Accounts

**Why**: Track income/expenses, tax preparation

**Action**: Open business bank account

---

### 3. Track Everything

**What to Track**:
- Income (all sources)
- Expenses
- Metrics
- Performance

**Tools**: Spreadsheet or accounting software

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Treat it as business** = professional approach
2. **Track everything** = better decisions
3. **Set goals** = clear direction
4. **Build systems** = sustainable growth
