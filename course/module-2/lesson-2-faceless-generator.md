## The Complete Workflow

### Step 1: Access the Feature

**Three Ways to Access**:

1. **Dashboard**: Click "Create Video" → "Faceless Video"
2. **Left Sidebar**: Click "Faceless Video Generator"
3. **Quick Create**: Top navigation "Create Video" button

**Pro Tip**: Bookmark the feature for quick access.

---

### Step 2: Choose Your Video Style

You have **4 powerful options**:

#### Option 1: AI Visuals (Most Popular) ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**What It Does**:
- AI generates custom images based on your script
- Images stitched into full video
- Most versatile option

**Best For**:
- Most content types
- Character consistency
- Custom visuals
- Educational content

**Credits**: 13 credits per 1-minute video

**When to Use**: Use this for 90% of your videos.

---

#### Option 2: Video Vault

**What It Does**:
- Pre-selected B-roll background videos
- One video stays consistent throughout
- Single visual theme

**Best For**:
- Consistent branding
- Background videos
- Simple content
- When you want one visual style

**Credits**: 13 credits per 1-minute video

**When to Use**: When you want a consistent background.

---

#### Option 3: B-Roll Videos

**What It Does**:
- Pulls relevant clips from stock library
- Automatically matches clips to script
- Professional visual experience

**Best For**:
- Professional look
- Variety in visuals
- Stock footage content
- Documentary style

**Credits**: 13 credits per 1-minute video

**When to Use**: When you want professional stock footage.

---

#### Option 4: URL to Video

**What It Does**:
- Scrapes images from any webpage
- Perfect for affiliate marketers
- **No credits required** for image scraping

**Best For**:
- Product promotions
- Real estate listings
- Affiliate marketing
- Website promotions

**Credits**: Only video generation (13 per minute)

**When to Use**: Promoting products or websites.

---

### Step 3: Schedule Setup

**Configure Publishing**:

1. **Video Title**: 
   - SEO-optimized
   - Include keywords
   - Make it compelling

2. **Description**:
   - Auto-generate (recommended) ✓
   - Or write custom
   - Include CTAs

3. **Tags**:
   - Auto-generate (recommended) ✓
   - Or add custom tags
   - Relevant keywords

4. **Hashtags**:
   - Platform-specific
   - Trending hashtags
   - Niche-relevant

5. **CTAs**:
   - Add affiliate links
   - Product links
   - Course links
   - Any promotion

6. **Publishing Options**:
   - **Publish Now**: Immediate
   - **Schedule for Later**: Use calendar
   - **Bulk Schedule**: Multiple videos

**Pro Tip**: Always enable "Auto-generate AI description and tags" for better SEO.

---

### Step 4: Video Customization

**Language**:
- Choose from 25+ languages
- Affects voiceover and subtitles
- Match your audience

**Voice Actor**:
- Multiple AI voices available
- Choose based on niche
- Test different voices
- Find your brand voice

**Subtitle Font & Style**:
- Customize appearance
- Match your brand
- Ensure readability
- Test different styles

**Video Length**:
- **Short-form**: Under 1 minute (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
- **Long-form**: 5-20 minutes (YouTube)
- Choose based on platform

**Aspect Ratio**:
- **Portrait**: 9:16 (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts)
- **Landscape**: 16:9 (YouTube, Facebook)
- **Square**: 1:1 (Instagram posts)

**Pro Tip**: Create both formats from same script for maximum reach.

---

### Step 5: Script Creation (Four Powerful Options)

#### Option 1: Custom Script

**When to Use**:
- You have existing content
- You want full control
- You have specific messaging

**How to Use**:
1. Click "Custom Script"
2. Paste or write your script
3. Review and edit
4. Continue to generation

---

#### Option 2: AI Script Assistant (Recommended) ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**When to Use**:
- 90% of your videos
- You have a topic but no script
- You want proven formulas

**How to Use**:
1. Click "AI Script Assistant"
2. Enter your topic (e.g., "How to use AI for content creation")
3. Click "Generate Script"
4. Review generated script
5. Edit if needed (optional)

**Why It's Powerful**:
- Trained on top-performing scripts
- Based on proven formulas
- Saves hours of writing
- Often better than manual scripts

**Pro Tip**: Let AI write first, then edit. It's faster and often better.

---

#### Option 3: URL to Script

**When to Use**:
- Repurposing blog posts
- Converting articles to videos
- Content repurposing strategy

**How to Use**:
1. Click "URL to Script"
2. Paste article or blog URL
3. Click "Generate Script from URL"
4. Review generated script
5. Edit if needed

**Pro Tip**: Perfect for content repurposing. One article = multiple videos.

---

#### Option 4: Audio to Video

**When to Use**:
- You have audio recordings
- Podcast repurposing
- Voiceover content
- Music videos

**How to Use**:
1. Click "Audio to Video"
2. Upload audio file
3. Syllaby generates visuals
4. Video created automatically

**Pro Tip**: Great for repurposing podcasts or audio content.

---

---

### Step 7: Generate Video

**Before Generating**:
- Review summary (length, credits)
- Check all settings
- Verify script
- Ensure everything is correct

**Generate Process**:
1. Click "Generate Video" (bottom right)
2. System renders in background
3. Wait 1-2 minutes
4. Video appears in "My Videos" section

**After Generation**:
- Syllaby shows **5 new topic ideas**
- Related to your video topic
- Click any idea to create next video
- Seamless content pipeline

**Pro Tip**: Use the suggested topics to build your content pipeline.

## Pro Tips for Mastery

### Content Creation

1. **Start with AI Script Assistant**: Saves time, proven formulas
2. **Test Different Styles**: See what resonates with audience
3. **Use Character Consistency**: Build brand recognition
4. **Optimize for Platform**: Different formats for different platforms

### Script Writing

1. **Hook in First 3 Seconds**: Critical for retention
2. **Value-First**: Every video should provide clear value
3. **Clear CTAs**: End with specific call to action
4. **Storytelling**: Use narratives to keep viewers engaged

### Optimization

1. **SEO Titles**: Use keywords in titles
2. **Descriptions**: Auto-generate for better SEO
3. **Tags**: Relevant tags for discoverability
4. **Thumbnails**: Create eye-catching thumbnails

### Efficiency

1. **Batch Create**: Create multiple videos at once
2. **Use Templates**: Save successful settings
3. **Auto-Generate**: Let AI handle descriptions
4. **Schedule Ahead**: Plan content in advance

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Overthinking**: Start simple, iterate
2. **Skipping Setup**: Proper setup saves time
3. **Ignoring SEO**: Auto-generate descriptions and tags
4. **Not Testing**: Test different styles and voices
5. **Perfectionism**: Done is better than perfect
