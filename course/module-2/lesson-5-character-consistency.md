**The Problem Without It**:

- Confusing for audience
- Hard to build following

**The Solution: Character Consistency**:
- Same character throughout
- Instant brand recognition
- Professional appearance
- Cohesive storytelling

**Character consistency can increase brand recognition by 3-5x.**

## How to Use Character Consistency

### Step 1: Choose Your Video Style

**Required**: Must use "AI Visuals" style

**Why**: Character consistency only works with AI Visuals currently

**Note**: More styles being added regularly

---

### Step 2: Select Supported Art Style

**Current Support**:
- ~10 art styles support character consistency
- Look for **purple icon** on styles
- Indicates character consistency available

**Supported Styles Include**:
- Realistic Cartoon
- Hyper Realism
- And more (check for purple icon)

**Future**: All styles will support it soon

---

### Step 3: Choose Your Character

**What You Get**:
- Full list of available characters
- Each with distinct look and personality
- Pre-trained and ready
- Consistent appearance

**How to Choose**:
1. Browse character options
2. Consider your niche
3. Think about brand
4. Select your character

**Examples**:
- "Theo" - Professional, friendly
- "Leo" - Adventurous, energetic
- And more options available

**Pro Tip**: Choose one character and use consistently across all videos.

---

### Step 4: Create Your Video

**Process**:
1. Select AI Visuals style
2. Choose supported art style (purple icon)
3. Select your character
4. Add script (custom or AI-generated)
5. Generate video

**Result**: Same character appears throughout entire video.
