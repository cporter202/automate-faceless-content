## Advanced Features Overview

[Syllaby.io](https://syllaby.io/?via=chris56) offers advanced features beyond basic video creation. These features help you create more sophisticated content and scale your business.

---

## Feature 1: URL to Video

### What It Does

**Functionality**:
- Scrapes images from any webpage
- Creates video from URL content
- Perfect for affiliate marketers
- **No credits for image scraping**

**How It Works**:
1. Paste URL of webpage
2. Syllaby analyzes page
3. Extracts images and content
4. Creates video automatically

**Best For**:
- Product promotions
- Real estate listings
- Affiliate marketing
- Website promotions

---

### Complete Workflow

**Step 1: Access Feature**:
- In Faceless Video Generator
- Select "URL to Video" style
- Paste URL

**Step 2: Generate Script**:
- Click "Generate Script from URL"
- AI analyzes page
- Creates complete script
- Ready to use

**Step 3: Create Video**:
- Follow normal video creation
- Images already scraped
- No credit cost for images
- Only pay for video generation

**Result**: Professional promotional video from any URL.

---

### Pro Tips

1. **Perfect for Affiliates**: Promote products without creating images
2. **Real Estate**: Turn listings into videos
3. **E-commerce**: Promote products automatically
4. **No Image Credits**: Saves credits on image generation

## Feature 2: Audio to Video

### What It Does

**Functionality**:
- Upload any audio file
- Generates visuals to match
- Creates complete video
- Perfect for repurposing

**How It Works**:
1. Upload audio file (voiceover, song, podcast)
2. Syllaby generates visuals
3. Matches audio to visuals
4. Creates complete video

**Best For**:
- Podcast repurposing
- Audio content conversion
- Music videos
- Voiceover content

---

### Complete Workflow

**Step 1: Access Feature**:
- In Faceless Video Generator
- Select "Audio to Video" option
- Upload audio file

**Step 2: Generate Video**:
- AI analyzes audio
- Generates matching visuals
- Creates complete video
- Automatic sync

**Step 3: Customize**:
- Edit visuals if needed
- Adjust timing
- Add effects
- Finalize video

**Result**: Video created from any audio.

---

### Pro Tips

1. **Repurpose Podcasts**: Turn audio into video content
2. **Music Videos**: Create visuals for songs
3. **Voiceovers**: Convert audio to video
4. **Time Savings**: No need to create visuals manually

## Feature 3: Text to Scene (Ultra-Realistic Videos)

### What It Does

**Functionality**:
- Creates ultra-realistic videos
- Uses advanced AI models
- Cinematic quality
- Professional production value

**Available Models**:
- **Sora 2 & Sora 2 Pro**: Ultra-realistic motion, shadows, cinematic depth
- **Google VEO 3**: Sharp character consistency, vibrant colors
- **Seedance Lite/Pro**: High-quality scene generation

**Best For**:
- Premium content
- Long-form videos
- Cinematic quality
- High-production value

---

### Model Comparison

**Sora 2**:
- **Cost**: 3 credits per second
- **Quality**: Ultra-realistic
- **Best For**: General high-quality content

**Sora 2 Pro**:
- **Cost**: 13 credits per second
- **Quality**: Highest quality
- **Best For**: Premium content

**Google VEO3 Fast**:
- **Cost**: 4 credits per second
- **Quality**: Fast generation
- **Best For**: Quick high-quality content

**Google VEO3 Quality**:
- **Cost**: 11 credits per second
- **Quality**: High quality
- **Best For**: Balanced quality/speed

**Seedance Lite**:
- **Cost**: ~1 credit per second
- **Quality**: Good quality
- **Best For**: Cost-effective quality

**Seedance Pro**:
- **Cost**: ~2 credits per second
- **Quality**: Better quality
- **Best For**: Better quality, still affordable

---

### When to Use Text to Scene

**Use For**:
- Premium long-form content
- Cinematic videos
- High-production value
- Special projects

**Don't Use For**:
- Daily content (too expensive)
- Short-form content (overkill)
- Testing ideas (use regular faceless)
- High-volume content

**Pro Tip**: Use for special content, regular faceless for daily posts.
