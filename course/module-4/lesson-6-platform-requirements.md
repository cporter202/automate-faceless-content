## Multi-Platform Monetization

While YouTube is primary, other platforms offer additional revenue streams. This lesson covers requirements for TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook.

---

## TikTok Monetization

### Creator Fund Requirements

**Basic Requirements**:
- **10,000 followers** (minimum)
- **100,000 video views** in past 30 days
- **18+ years old**
- Meet Community Guidelines
- Live in eligible country (US, UK, France, Germany, Italy, Spain)

**Creator Fund 2.0**:
- **50,000 followers** (minimum)
- **500,000 views** in past 30 days
- Higher earning potential

---

### TikTok Revenue Streams

**1. Creator Fund**:
- Earnings: $0.02-0.04 per 1,000 views (very low)
- Payment: Monthly
- Based on views and engagement
- **Note**: Not main income source

**2. TikTok Creator Marketplace**:
- Brand partnerships
- **Requirements**: 10,000+ followers
- **Rates**: $100-5,000+ per post
- **Formula**: $0.01-0.05 per follower

**3. Live Gifts**:
- Viewers send virtual gifts
- **Requirements**: 1,000+ followers, 18+
- **Earnings**: 50% of gift value
- **Potential**: $50-500+ per live stream

**4. TikTok Shop (Affiliate)**:
- Promote products in videos
- **Requirements**: 1,000+ followers
- **Commission**: 5-20% per sale

**5. Series (Premium Content)**:
- Charge for exclusive content
- **Requirements**: 10,000+ followers
- **Price**: $0.99-19.99 (you set)
- **Potential**: $100-1,000+ per series

---

### How to Get Monetized on TikTok Fast

**Strategy**:
- Post 3-5 videos per day
- Use trending sounds and hashtags
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Post at optimal times (6-10 AM, 7-9 PM)
- Engage with comments immediately
- Cross-post from other platforms

**Timeline**: 2-4 weeks to 10,000 followers with consistent posting

## Instagram Monetization

### Requirements

**Reels Bonus Program**:
- **10,000 followers** OR **1,000+ Reels views** in past 30 days
- Live in eligible country
- Follow Community Guidelines

**Instagram Subscriptions**:
- **10,000 followers** (minimum)
- Exclusive content for subscribers
- **Price**: $0.99-99.99/month (you set)

**Branded Content**:
- **1,000+ followers** (minimum)
- Sponsored posts and partnerships
- Must disclose sponsored content

---

### Instagram Revenue Streams

**1. Reels Bonus Program**:
- **Earnings**: $600-35,000 per month (varies)
- Based on Reels performance
- **Requirements**: Invite-only, high engagement

**2. Instagram Subscriptions**:
- **Price**: Set your own ($0.99-99.99/month)
- **Potential**: 50-200 subscribers = $50-2,000/month
- Exclusive content, lives, stories

**3. Brand Partnerships**:
- **Rates**: $100-10,000+ per post
- **Formula**: $0.01-0.10 per follower
- Sponsored posts, stories, reels

**4. Affiliate Marketing**:
- Promote products in posts/stories
- **Commission**: 5-30% per sale
- Use affiliate links in bio

**5. Instagram Shopping**:
- Sell products directly from posts
- **Requirements**: Business account, eligible country
- Connect to Facebook Shop

---

### How to Get Monetized on Instagram Fast

**Strategy**:
- Post 1-2 Reels per day
- Use trending audio and effects
- Post at optimal times (11 AM-1 PM, 7-9 PM)
- Engage with comments and DMs
- Use relevant hashtags (5-10 per post)
- Cross-promote from other platforms

**Timeline**: 2-6 weeks to 10,000 followers with consistent Reels

**Pro Tip**: Use [Syllaby.io](https://syllaby.io/?via=chris56) to create Reels content.

## Facebook Monetization

### Requirements

**In-Stream Ads**:
- **10,000 followers** (minimum)
- **600,000 total minutes viewed** in past 60 days
- **5+ active videos** in past 14 days
- Live in eligible country

**Stars (Live Gifts)**:
- **1,000+ followers** (minimum)
- Viewers send stars during live streams
- **Earnings**: $0.01 per star

**Fan Subscriptions**:
- **10,000 followers** (minimum)
- **OR 250+ return viewers** in past 60 days
- Monthly subscription model
- **Price**: $4.99-29.99/month

---

### Facebook Revenue Streams

**1. In-Stream Ads**:
- Ads shown in longer videos (3+ minutes)
- **CPM**: $1-5 per 1,000 views
- **Requirements**: 10,000+ followers, 600K minutes

**2. Stars (Live Gifts)**:
- Viewers send stars during lives
- **Earnings**: $0.01 per star
- **Potential**: $50-500+ per live stream

**3. Fan Subscriptions**:
- Monthly recurring revenue
- **Price**: $4.99-29.99/month
- **Potential**: 50-200 subscribers = $250-6,000/month

**4. Brand Partnerships**:
- Sponsored content
- **Rates**: $100-5,000+ per post
- Facebook Creator Marketplace

**5. Affiliate Marketing**:
- Promote products in videos
- **Commission**: 5-20% per sale

---

### How to Get Monetized on Facebook Fast

**Strategy**:
- Post 1-2 videos per day
- Focus on longer content (3+ minutes)
- Go live regularly (builds followers faster)
- Engage with comments
- Join relevant groups
- Cross-promote from other platforms

**Timeline**: 1-3 months to 10,000 followers with consistent posting
