# Lesson 6: Micro SaaS Products

**Duration**: 30 minutes  
**Goal**: Build recurring revenue with Micro SaaS products

---

## What is Micro SaaS?

**Definition**: Single-purpose software that solves one specific problem

**Characteristics**:
- One clear function
- Simple to use
- Affordable pricing
- Recurring revenue

**Examples**:
- Bible study tool ($5/month)
- Niche calculators
- Automation tools
- Educational platforms

**Why It Works**: Recurring revenue = predictable income.

---

## Why Micro SaaS is Powerful

### The Recurring Revenue Advantage

**Traditional Products**:
- One-time sale
- Need new customers constantly
- Unpredictable income
- Hard to scale

**Micro SaaS**:
- Monthly subscription
- Recurring revenue
- Predictable income
- Scales with subscribers

**Example**:
- 200 subscribers × $5/month = $1,000/month
- Recurring = predictable
- Grows over time
- Passive income

**Pro Tip**: Recurring revenue is the holy grail of online business.

## How to Build Micro SaaS

### Step 1: Identify Problem

**Questions**:
- What problem exists in your niche?
- What do people struggle with?
- What would save time?
- What would provide value?

**Example** (Religious Niche):
- Problem: Need daily Bible study
- Solution: Daily Bible study tool
- Value: Convenience, consistency

**Pro Tip**: Solve problems you understand.

---

### Step 2: Build Solution

**Platforms**:
- **Lovable**: No-code platform
- **Replit**: Code-based
- **Bubble**: Visual builder
- **Webflow**: Design-focused

**Time Required**: 10-40 hours (one-time)

**Cost**: $0-50/month (hosting)

**Pro Tip**: Start with no-code - faster and easier.

---

### Step 3: Set Pricing

**Pricing Strategy**:
- **Low**: $5-10/month (more subscribers)
- **Medium**: $15-25/month (balanced)
- **High**: $30-50/month (premium)

**Recommendation**: Start at $5-10/month, increase as value proves.

---

**How to Launch**:
1. Create landing page
