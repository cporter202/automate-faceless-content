### Frequency Guidelines

**Beginners** (Months 1-3):
- Minimum: 1 video/day
- Optimal: 2 videos/day
- Focus: Consistency over perfection

**Intermediate** (Months 4-6):
- Minimum: 1 video/day
- Optimal: 2-3 videos/day
- Focus: Quality + quantity

**Advanced** (Months 7+):
- Minimum: 2 videos/day
- Optimal: 3-5 videos/day
- Focus: Scale and optimize

**Pro Tip**: Start with 1/day, increase as you scale.
