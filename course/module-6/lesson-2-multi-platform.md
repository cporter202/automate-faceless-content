## Platform-Specific Optimization

### YouTube Optimization

**Content**: Long-form (10-20 minutes)
**Focus**: SEO, watch time, revenue
**Strategy**: Comprehensive content, series

**Pro Tip**: Primary platform - invest most effort here.

---

### TikTok Optimization

**Content**: Short-form (15-60 seconds)
**Focus**: Trends, engagement, growth
**Strategy**: Hook in first 3 seconds, trending sounds

**Pro Tip**: Use for fast growth, drive to YouTube.

---

### Instagram Optimization

**Content**: Reels (15-90 seconds)
**Focus**: Visual, story-driven
**Strategy**: High-quality visuals, engaging stories

**Pro Tip**: Visual-first content performs best.

---

### Facebook Optimization

**Content**: Long-form (3+ minutes)
**Focus**: Community, engagement
**Strategy**: Longer content, community building

**Pro Tip**: Great for building engaged community.

---

## Content Repurposing Strategy

### One Video = Multiple Platforms

**Long-Form to Shorts**:
- Extract 5-10 key moments
- Create Shorts from moments
- Link to long-form
- Cross-promote

**Shorts to Long-Form**:
- Expand popular Shorts
- Deep dive on topic
- Link from Short
- Drive traffic

**Result**: Maximum ROI from every piece of content.
