# Lesson 3: Content Repurposing for Maximum ROI

**Duration**: 20 minutes  
**Goal**: Turn one piece of content into 6-11 pieces across platforms

---

## The Repurposing System

**One Long-Form Video** can become:
- 1 YouTube long-form
- 5-10 YouTube Shorts
- 5-10 TikTok videos
- 5-10 Instagram Reels
- 1-3 Facebook posts
- Blog post
- Email content
- Social media posts

**Result**: 1 video = 6-11+ pieces of content.

---

## Repurposing Workflow

### Step 1: Create Long-Form

**Using [Syllaby.io](https://syllaby.io/?via=chris56)**:
- Create 10-20 minute video
- Comprehensive content
- All key points covered
- High value

**This is your source content.**

---

### Step 2: Extract Shorts

**How**:
- Identify 5-10 key moments
- Each = 15-60 second Short
- Create using [Syllaby.io](https://syllaby.io/?via=chris56)
- Link to long-form

**Result**: 5-10 Shorts from one video.

---

### Step 3: Adapt for Each Platform

**TikTok**: Hook in first 3 seconds, trending sounds
**Instagram**: Visual-first, story-driven
**Facebook**: Longer clips, community focus
**YouTube Shorts**: Link to long-form

**Result**: Platform-optimized content.
