# Lesson 3: Team Building & Outsourcing

**Duration**: 30 minutes  
**Goal**: Build team to scale beyond personal capacity

---

## When to Build a Team

**Signs You Need Help**:
- Can't keep up with demand
- Revenue justifies team
- Want to scale faster
- Ready to delegate

**Benefits**:
- Scale beyond personal capacity
- Focus on high-value tasks
- Faster growth
- Sustainable business

---

## Team Roles

### 1. Content Creator

**Role**: Create videos using [Syllaby.io](https://syllaby.io/?via=chris56)

**Cost**: $500-2,000/month

**Benefit**: 10x content output

---

### 2. Editor

**Role**: Edit videos, optimize

**Cost**: $300-1,000/month

**Benefit**: Better quality, more time

---

### 3. Social Media Manager

**Role**: Manage all platforms

**Cost**: $400-1,500/month

**Benefit**: Consistent posting, engagement

---

### 4. VA (Virtual Assistant)

**Role**: Admin tasks, research

**Cost**: $200-800/month

**Benefit**: Time back for high-value work

---

## Hiring Process

### Step 1: Identify Needs

**Questions**:
- What takes most time?
- What can be delegated?
- What's highest ROI to outsource?

**Result**: Clear hiring priorities

---

### Step 2: Find Talent

**Where**:
- Upwork
- Fiverr
- Facebook groups
- Referrals

**Pro Tip**: Start with one person, expand gradually.

---

### Step 3: Onboard

**Process**:
- Clear instructions
- Systems documentation
- Training
- Regular check-ins

**Result**: Effective team member

---

## Pro Tips
