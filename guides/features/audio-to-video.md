# Audio to Video

Convert any audio into professional video content - perfect for repurposing podcasts and audio content.

---

## What is Audio to Video?

**Definition**: Upload any audio file and Syllaby generates visuals to match, creating complete video

**Perfect For**: Podcast repurposing, audio content conversion, music videos, voiceover content

**Result**: Video created from any audio

---

## How It Works

### The Process

1. Upload audio file (voiceover, song, podcast)
2. Syllaby generates visuals
3. Matches audio to visuals
4. Creates complete video
5. Automatic sync

**Result**: Professional video from any audio source

## Complete Workflow

### Step 1: Access Feature

**Where to Find**:
- In Faceless Video Generator
- Select "Audio to Video" option
- Upload audio file

**Supported Formats**: Most common audio formats

### Step 2: Generate Video

**Process**:
1. AI analyzes audio
2. Generates matching visuals
3. Creates complete video
4. Automatic sync

### Step 3: Customize (Optional)

**What You Can Do**:
