# Revenue Streams Explained

Complete breakdown of all revenue streams available to faceless video creators.

---

## The Revenue Stack

**Five Income Streams**:
1. YouTube AdSense (Base Income)
2. Affiliate Marketing (High Potential)
3. Digital Products (Recurring Sales)
4. Brand Deals (Premium Rates)
5. Micro SaaS (Recurring Revenue)

**Total Potential**: $1,000-10,000+/month

---

## Stream 1: YouTube AdSense

**What It Is**: Ads shown on your videos

**Earnings**: $2-50 per 1,000 views

**Requirements**: 1,000 subscribers, 4,000 watch hours

**How to Maximize**:
- Longer videos (more ad slots)
- Higher retention (more ads shown)
- High-CPM niches
- Valuable audiences

**Expected**: $100-1,500/month (starting)

---

## Stream 2: Affiliate Marketing

**What It Is**: Recommend products, earn commission

**Earnings**: $50-500 per 1,000 views (with conversion)

**Requirements**: None (join programs)

**How to Maximize**:
- High-commission products
- Comparison videos
- Build trust
- Track conversions

**Expected**: $200-1,500/month

---

## Stream 3: Digital Products

**What It Is**: Courses, ebooks, templates

**Earnings**: $100-2,000+/month per product

**Requirements**: Create and sell

**How to Maximize**:
- Valuable products
- Video promotion
- Email list
- Bundle products

**Expected**: $300-1,000/month

---

## Stream 4: Brand Deals

**What It Is**: Brands pay you to promote

**Earnings**: $100-10,000+ per deal

**Requirements**: 5,000-10,000+ subscribers

**How to Maximize**:
- Engaged audience
- Media kit
- Marketplaces
- Relationships

**Expected**: $500-2,000/month

---

## Stream 5: Micro SaaS

**What It Is**: Recurring subscription products

**Earnings**: $500-5,000+/month recurring

**Requirements**: Build product, promote

**How to Maximize**:
- Solve problems
- Multi-channel promotion
- Email list
- Value focus

**Expected**: $500-2,000/month

---

## Scaling Strategy

### Month 1-3: Foundation
- AdSense: $100-300
- Affiliates: $50-200
- **Total**: $150-500

### Month 4-6: Expansion
- AdSense: $200-500
- Affiliates: $200-500
- Products: $100-500
- **Total**: $500-1,500

### Month 7-12: Scaling
- All streams active
- **Total**: $1,000-10,000+

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Start with AdSense + Affiliates** = foundation
2. **Add products gradually** = build over time
3. **All streams active** = maximum income
4. **Track everything** = optimize performance
