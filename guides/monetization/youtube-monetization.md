# YouTube Monetization

Complete guide to monetizing on YouTube with faceless video content.

---

## YouTube Partner Program Requirements

### Standard Requirements

**Subscribers**: 1,000 minimum  
**Watch Hours**: 4,000 hours in past 12 months (OR 10M Shorts views)  
**Community Guidelines**: Must be compliant  
**Eligible Country**: Must be in eligible location

---

## Getting Monetized Fast

### Strategy 1: Focus on Watch Time

**How**:
- Create longer videos (10-20 minutes)
- Improve retention
- Create series content
- Create U-log videos

**Result**: Faster to 4,000 hours

---

### Strategy 2: Multi-Channel Approach

**How**:
- Create 3-5 channels
- Combine watch hours
- Cross-promote
- Faster overall growth

**Result**: Cut monetization time in half

---

### Strategy 3: Shorts + Long-Form

**How**:
- Shorts for subscribers
- Long-form for watch time
- Cross-promote
- Balanced strategy

**Result**: Fast growth + watch time

## AdSense Optimization

### Maximize CPM

**High-CPM Niches**:
- Finance: $10-50 per 1,000 views
- Business: $8-25 per 1,000 views
- Technology: $5-20 per 1,000 views

**How to Increase**:
- Target valuable audiences
- Create high-value content
- Improve retention
- Longer videos

---

### Ad Placement

**Long-Form Videos**:
- Pre-roll (before video)
- Mid-roll (every 8 minutes)
- Post-roll (after video)

**Optimization**:
- 8+ minute videos get mid-roll
- 10+ minute videos get multiple mid-rolls
- More ad slots = more revenue

---

## Pro Tips
