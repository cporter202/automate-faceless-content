## Complete Workflow

### Step 1: Join Affiliate Programs (30 minutes)

**Programs to Join**:
- Amazon Associates (easy, everything)
- Software affiliates (20-50% commission)
- Course affiliates (30-50% commission)
- Niche-specific programs

**How to Join**:
1. Research programs in your niche
2. Apply to 5-10 programs
3. Get affiliate links
4. Set up tracking

**Pro Tip**: Start with Amazon (easy) + 2-3 software programs (high commission).

---

### Step 2: Find Products to Promote (30 minutes)

**What to Look For**:
- Products you use and believe in
- High-commission products
- Products in your niche
- Products with good reviews

**How to Find**:
- Browse affiliate networks
- Check product websites
- Research in your niche
- Test products yourself

**Pro Tip**: Start with products you actually use - authentic recommendations convert better.

---

### Step 3: Create Affiliate Video (5 minutes)

**Using [Syllaby.io URL to Video](https://syllaby.io/?via=chris56)**:

**Method 1: URL to Video** (Fastest):
1. Paste product URL
2. Click "Generate Script from URL"
3. AI creates complete script
4. Configure video settings
5. Generate video
6. Add affiliate link in description
7. Publish

**Method 2: AI Script Assistant**:
1. Enter: "Review of [Product]" or "Best [Product Type]"
2. AI generates review script
3. Create video
4. Add affiliate link
5. Publish

**Time**: 5 minutes per video

**Result**: Professional affiliate video ready.

---

### Step 4: Add Affiliate Links

**Where to Place**:
1. **Video Description** (Primary):
   - Clear call-to-action
   - "Check link in description"
   - Trackable link

2. **Mention in Video**:
   - Verbal mention
   - "Link in description"
   - Multiple mentions

3. **Pinned Comment**:
   - Always visible
   - Easy to click

4. **End Screen** (YouTube):
   - Visual CTA
   - Link to product

**Pro Tip**: Use all placements for maximum conversions.

---

### Step 5: Track and Optimize

**What to Track**:
- Click-through rates
- Conversion rates
- Revenue per video
- Top-performing products

**How to Optimize**:
- Test different approaches
- Double down on winners
- Improve CTAs
- Optimize based on data

**Pro Tip**: 1-5% conversion rate is good - track and improve.
